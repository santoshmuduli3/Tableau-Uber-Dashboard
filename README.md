# Tableau-Uber-Dashboard

**Key Business Insights**
1. Strong Overall Business-148K bookings generated over ₹51M in revenue.
2. Stable Demand Throughout the Year-Monthly bookings and revenue remain consistent with only minor seasonal variation.
3. Evening Commute Drives Maximum Demand-Peak bookings occur during 5 PM–8 PM.
4. Demand Is Evenly Spread Across the Week-No single weekday dominates bookings.
5. Central Pickup Locations Generate the Highest Revenue-Barakhamba Road and nearby locations are major booking hubs.
6. Most Customers Travel Short-to-Medium Distances-Trips under 20 km make up the majority of bookings.
7. Average Fare Is Consistent-Average booking values remain around ₹500 regardless of trip distance, indicating effective pricing consistency.

**Business Recommendations**
1. Increase driver availability during morning (9–11 AM) and evening (5–8 PM) peak hours to reduce wait times.
2. Deploy additional drivers around Barakhamba Road, Khandsa, and other high-demand pickup locations.
3. Promote ride discounts during off-peak hours (midnight–4 AM and late evenings) to improve utilization.
4. Encourage longer-distance rides with loyalty rewards or bundled offers to increase revenue per trip.
5. Use demand forecasting to optimize driver allocation during high-demand months such as July.
6. Analyze pricing for longer-distance trips to ensure profitability while maintaining customer satisfaction.
